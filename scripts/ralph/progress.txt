- `calculateThermalVelocity` canonical location is `src/lib/weather-profile.ts`; re-exported from `thermal-cloud-utils.ts` for windgram consumers

- Pressure levels array is ordered by PRESSURE_LEVELS constant (1000â†’500 hPa, i.e., lowest altitude first)
- Scoring v2 uses `calculateDailyScoresFromProfile(profile, forecast, site)` â€” callers should try v2 with fallback to v1 `calculateDailyScores`
- Wind directions: use `getIdealWindDirections(site)` and `getOrientations(site)` from `src/lib/site-utils.ts` to derive from wind columns
- LaunchSite lat/lng are `number` (real), customSites lat/lng are still `string` (text) â€” no parseFloat needed for launch sites
